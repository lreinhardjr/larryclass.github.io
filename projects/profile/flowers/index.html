Purchasing Flowers
5
With produce in hand, we head over to Market Blooms for flowers.
Lilies or chrysanthemums, tulips or roses, it’s impossible not to find something beautiful at Market Blooms, Reading Terminal Market’s resident florist.
Their selection includes fresh flowers from all over the world, but what makes Market Blooms truly special is that, during the local growing season, owner Sidney Poor sources many of the stand’s flowers from former owner Steve Deshong’s farm in Clarksburg, NJ.
Here, we can enjoy a simple arrangement of local peonies, sunflowers, zinnias, and many other flowers and greens – grown using organic and sustainable practices wherever possible – or put together a more exotic arrangement of birds of paradise or orchids.
