Market

As you enter the market you are immediately hit with a sensory overload of voluble conversation, mouthwatering smells and flamboyant colors.
The gastronomic bazaar that was originally built almost 150 years ago is home to more than 80 merchants.
On any given day one can find an eclectic array of fresh baked Amish goods, produce direct from the field, unusual spices, free range meats and poultry, flowers, ethnic foods, and much more.
