One of the biggest adventures of visiting the Reading Terminal market is finding a place to sit to enjoy your food.
Because of the markets popularity, tables are hard to come by and visitors engage in a friendly banter for who can sit to eat their delicious food the quickest.
As you sit to enjoy your food, you embark on a new sensory experience that is much more than just taste.
The seating area is in the middle of the market, you can smell the local Aztec churros, hear orders being called out at the philly cheese steak stand and watch as visitors eye your table for their own opportunity to enjoy the marketâ€™s offering.
